**0verview:**

British American Tobacco Zimbabwe (Holdings) Limited, together with its subsidiaries, manufactures, distributes, and sells cigarettes in Zimbabwe. The company also exports cut rags. It offers the products under the Dunhill, Luck Strike, and Rothmans brands. The company sells its products through a network of independent retailers, wholesalers, and distributors. British American Tobacco Zimbabwe (Holdings) Limited is headquartered in Harare, Zimbabwe.
